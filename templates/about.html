About This Tool

Built by Nova Technologies to make professional image editing free for everyone.

What Is AI Image Object Remover?

AI Image Object Remover is a free, browser-based tool that uses advanced AI inpainting technology to remove any unwanted element from your photos — watermarks, logos, text overlays, buttons, people, objects, or anything else you want gone.

Simply upload your image, paint over the area you want removed, and our AI reconstructs the background seamlessly — in seconds, at no cost.

How It Works

  1. Upload your image (JPEG, PNG, or WebP up to 20MB).
  2. Paint over the object or watermark you want removed using our brush tool.
  3. Download the clean result. The AI fills in the area using surrounding pixel context.

The underlying technology is OpenCV TELEA inpainting — the same algorithm used in professional photo restoration software. It analyses the pixels surrounding the painted area and reconstructs what should be there.

Why Free?

We believe useful tools should be accessible to everyone. The tool is supported by advertising (Google AdSense), which lets us keep it completely free with no sign-up, no watermarks on your output, and no limits.
