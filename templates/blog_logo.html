How to Remove a Logo or Brand Mark from Any Photo (Free)

By Nova Technologies · March 2026 · 4 min read

Product photos, screenshots, stock images — logos show up everywhere. Whether it's a brand watermark, a TV channel bug, a website button, or a company logo stamped on a photo, our free AI tool removes it cleanly and fills in the background.

When Would You Need to Remove a Logo?

  • Removing a TV station bug from a screenshot
  • Cleaning up product photos with brand stamps
  • Removing website UI elements (buttons, banners) from screenshots
  • Stripping photographer watermarks from your own photos
  • Removing social media icons overlaid on images

How to Remove a Logo in 3 Steps

1. Open the Tool and Upload

Visit AI Image Object Remover and upload your image. No account required.

2. Brush Over the Logo

Use the red brush to paint over the logo. For sharp-edged logos, paint precisely around the edges. For logos with shadows or glows, include those in your painted area too. Use the brush size slider to get precision.

3. Download the Clean Result

Hit Remove Object and the AI fills in the logo area with reconstructed background. Download your clean image as a PNG.

Tips for Clean Logo Removal

  • Simple backgrounds give the best results — solid colours, sky, walls, grass.
  • Paint slightly beyond the logo edges to avoid artefacts at the boundary.
  • Multiple passes — if the result isn't perfect, download it, re-upload and paint the remaining traces.
  • For logos on complex textures (fabric, wood, skin), try a larger brush radius.

What About Large Logos?

Logos covering a large portion of the image are harder to remove perfectly because the AI has less surrounding context to work with. For these cases, the result will still remove the logo but may show some texture inconsistency. A second pass usually cleans it up further.
