How to Remove Text from a Photo for Free

By Nova Technologies · March 2026 · 4 min read

Text overlaid on photos — timestamps, date stamps, subtitles, captions, photographer credits, meme text — can ruin an otherwise great image. This free tool removes any text from photos by reconstructing the background underneath using AI.

Common Text Removal Use Cases

  • Date/time stamps from old camera photos
  • Subtitles or captions burned into video screenshots
  • Meme text you want to repurpose the image without
  • Photographer credits overlaid on stock photos
  • Price tags or labels on product images
  • Social media usernames watermarked on screenshots

Step-by-Step: Remove Text from a Photo

Step 1: Upload Your Image

Go to AI Image Object Remover and upload your photo (JPEG, PNG, or WebP, up to 20MB).

Step 2: Paint Over the Text

The brush tool paints a red overlay on the canvas. Cover all the text you want removed. For thin text (like a small timestamp), use a small brush and trace carefully. For large bold text, use a larger brush size.

Key tips for text removal:

  • Include any drop shadow or outline around the text in your painted area.
  • For multi-line text, paint all lines in one go before clicking Remove.
  • Paint slightly beyond the text bounding box on all sides.

Step 3: Remove and Download

Click Remove Object. The AI reconstructs the background behind the text. Switch to the Compare tab to see the before/after difference, then download.

Best Results for Text Removal

Text removal quality depends heavily on the background behind the text:

  • ✅ Best: Text on solid colour, sky, grass, simple patterns
  • ✅ Good: Text on slightly textured backgrounds (walls, fabric)
  • ⚠️ Harder: Text on faces, complex scenes, or other text

For difficult cases, try a second pass — download the result, re-upload, and paint any remaining traces.
